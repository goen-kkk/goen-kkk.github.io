Riding high

2021-05-20
English

文章来源： 《经济学人》 Apr 10th 2021 期

In the rich world the era of sharp-edged capitalism is giving way to a golden age for labour.

  • give way to : 让位于，为…替代，听凭决定

  • golden : 幸福美好的，非常成功的

    非常生动形象，可以做为happiness, successful的替换词。


The rich world’s workers endured competition from trade, relentless technological change, more unequal wages and tepid recoveries from recessions.

  • relentless: 不间断的，没完没了的
    • sth bad that is relentless continues without ever stopping or getting less severe
    • 严格的；无情的；坚决的
      • her relentless determination to succeed.
  • tepid: 不温不火的
    • tepid liquid is slightly warm, especially in a way that seems unpleasant.
    • （感情，反应）不够热情的，冷淡的

A reversal of the primacy of capital over labour beckons–and it will come sooner than you think.

  • beckon: 可能发生
    • [招手]示意
    • 招手[指机会等吸引人]，吸引
      • f sth such as a place or opportunity beckons, it appears so attractive that you want to have it

the labour market is beating forecasts as economies adapt to virus-containment measures.

  • beating: 超出（预期）
    • 击败，战胜
    • 击打
    • 打破（记录）
    • 搅拌
    • 克服，战胜
  • virus-containment measures: 疫情防控措施

As the labour market recovers, two deeper shifts are unfolding, in politics and in technology.

  • unfold：逐渐明朗；展现；打开

Robots will create armies of the idle, precarious jobs are displacing stable ones and even prosperous workers chained to emails and screens know in their hearts that their “bullshit jobs” are pointless.

  • （观点）机器人的普遍应用将是很多人无所事事、不稳定的工作正取代稳定的工作
  • be chained to：被…拴住
  • know in one’s heart：打心底知道
  • pointless：无意义的

Countries with the most automation, like Japan, enjoy some of the lowest unemployment.

  • automation：自动化程度

观点：The long-term future of work has changed for the better this year because it has become more digitised. Remote working is easing the bottleneck of expensive housing in thriving cities. Home-workers report higher levels of happiness and productivity.

this burst of technological investment to bring about faster productivity growth, which means higher wages.

  • bring about：导致，引起
