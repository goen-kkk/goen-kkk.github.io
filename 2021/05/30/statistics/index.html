Norway shows the scale of salmon farmers' Brexit problems

2021-05-30
English

文章来源： 《经济学人》 May 1st 2021 期


Salmon is a fancy dish, the kind of food people often order when dining out rather than cook at home.

  • fancy：繁复的，装饰的
    • decorative or complicated

Then came Brexit. As recently as December Scottish-farmed fish could move seamlessly to plates the length and breadth of the European Union (eu) but now they are subject to sanitary and phytosanitary (sps) checks at the border to ensure they meet eu standards.

  • 经典倒装句式，… come …
  • seamlessly：无缝地
    • without a break; smoothly
  • sanitary：adj.卫生的；环境卫生的；公共卫生的；清洁的
    • means concerned with keeping things clean and healthy, especially by providing a sewage system and a clean water supply

In January British salmon exports to the EU fell to just $0.7m, a 98% drop from January 2020. Figures published on April 26th showed that February brought some relief, with the value of salmon exports recovering to 25.6m, but they were still down by more than 10% on the year before.

This is really an exemplay sentence, if you’re preparing for any writing exams, especially for IELTS.


Disentangling the effects of the pandemic and Brexit is far from straightforward, but a clue lies across the North Sea.

  • disentangle：使脱离；使摆脱；分开；理顺，分清
    • to separate things that have become joined or confused
    • It's difficult to disentangle hard fact from myth, or truth from lies.
  • sth lie (in) 经典句型

In February the value of Norwegian exports fell by just 5% year on year, half the drop experienced in Britain.

  • Norwegian：
  • year on year
  • experience

January’s catastrophic fall in salmon exports reflected the difficulties of moving from a seamless border to a much knottier one.

  • knotty : complicated and difficult to solve.

Customs officials and haulier struggled to get to grips with new procedures and untested IT systems took time to bed down.

  • get/come to grips with 学习
    • taking time to understand something.
  • bed down：适应，渐入佳境

Exporters complain that different EU states are applying the rules with varying degrees of stringency, further complicating the task of complying.

  • stringency：严格程度
    • how strict you are with the rules
  • 也增加了遵守规定的难度

Those teething problems have begun to ease but there is still structural friction.

  • teething problems：类似长牙时的阵痛/早期时出现的问题

(partly) because that / because of the fact that
